  • Step 1: Grab Your Trezor Device

    To begin, grab your Trezor hardware wallet—whether it's the Trezor Model One or Trezor Model T. These devices are designed to keep your crypto assets safe from online threats. Plug your Trezor device into your computer or mobile device and you're ready to go.

  • Step 2: Visit Trezor.io/start

    Head over to Trezor.io/start to download Trezor Suite, the software that will guide you through the setup process. Trezor Suite is your all-in-one solution for managing your crypto: from sending and receiving coins to tracking your portfolio and even trading assets. No tech jargon—just a clean, intuitive interface that makes crypto management simple for everyone.

  • Step 3: Set Up Trezor Suite

    Once Trezor Suite is installed, open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to connect your Trezor device. The setup process is quick and easy, helping you securely create a wallet and back up your recovery phrase. Trezor Suite takes care of the technical details so you don’t have to.
